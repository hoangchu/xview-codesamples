-------------------------------------------------
Template Naming Convention
-------------------------------------------------
1. Pascal Casing: capitalize the first letter of each word 
   (including acronyms of over two letters in length)
2. Words can be separated by a space character
3. Optional "is-a" relationship character ":" is allowed.  
   For example "System: Script", means System "is-a" Script
4. Words on both the left and right sides of the ":" character
   must comply with the first two rules
5. Allowed characters are: A-Z, a-z, 0-9, :, space
